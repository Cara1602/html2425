Besides his patents, he published several dozen scientific articles, reviews, and a 396-page autobiography titled From Immigrant to Inventor (Scribner's, 1923). He won the annual Pulitzer Prize for Biography or Autobiography. It was later published in Serbian in 1929 under the title From Pastures to Scientist (Od pašnjaka do naučenjaka).
